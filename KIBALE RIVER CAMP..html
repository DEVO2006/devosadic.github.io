KIBALE RIVER CAMP


   Kibale River Camp

camp activities

 Kibale River Camp is a 3 star mid-range accommodation found at the edge of Kibale forest National Park (The home to thousand chimpanzees) in western part of Uganda.

   The camp is set outside the park in the perfect base for Chimpanzees Tracking and surrounded by the lush green landscape overlooking the majestic Rwenzori Range.

   The camp is convenient for relaxation and safari adventure to tourists, travellers and holiday makers who want to explore and discover the three national parks in Uganda

 name; Semiliki, Mt Rwenzori, and Queen Elizabeth national park with the beautiful views. You can be at the lodge by road transport from Kampala/Entebbe which takes

 approximately 5-6 hour's drive and an hour to fly from Entebbe International Airport to Kasese airstrip then hours to be transferred to the Resort.

Accomodation

Kibale River Camp consists of 8 elegant and comfortable cottages overlooking the breathtaking views of the lake reflecting African traditional style with natural materials with

 timbers.

   Tents are well elevated with raised wooden platform decorated and furnished with en-suite private facilities of clean flushing Toilets, bathroom with hot and cold showers,

 comfortable beds that are wrapped with mosquito nets, chairs, free tolletries, complementary bottled water in the rooms, white pillows and duvets and many more.

   Rooms have large sitting areas, terraces and private balconles overlooking the Lush green landscape and lakes. Rooms varles in different sizes with single beds for individuals,

 double beds for friends or couples, twin as well as family rooming option with different costs ranging from 200$ per night and above. Rates vary depending on seasons, during

 peak season the rate always go high than low season. Reservation Page

Kibale River camp offers different facilities and services which include;

On-site restaurant that serves delicious types of Menu WITH culinary traditional from all corners of the world and international meals that meets guests appetite. You will have

 Continental/buffet, breakfast lunch and a 3 course dinner in the main building served by well trained, organized staff and skilled chef.

A well-stocked bar with a shared lounge, you sip any drink of your choice especially in the evening when winding up overlooking the magnificent views of the surrounding lake.

Services include; Free parking space available, Laundry and house-keeping services available, airport pick-ups and drop offs offered, massage services offered, honey moon

 packages are offered.

WIFI hot spot internet available at the lodge that enables visitors to keep in touch with their families and friends.

Activities to do while at the lodge includes: birding/bird watching as you listen to the sound of the whispering birds in the village, Chimpanze tracking since Kibale is the home of

 chimpanzees, Hiking/climbing Mount Rwenzori, nature walks in the forest, and much more.
