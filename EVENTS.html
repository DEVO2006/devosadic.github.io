Message from THE SECRETARY

THE TESY JOURNEY SINCE INCEPTION TO DATE

           On 28th April 2023 around 4 pm, I received a call from Mr. Kasande Paul Regional team leader of Private Sector Foundation Uganda inviting me for a meeting at 

           Bishop Magambo Counsel Institute Virika.

  I was on site when the called but as my habit has been, I swing to action whenever I sense an opportunity to be useful in some way. He was apologetic having invited me on short  notice.

  I however made my commitment to be there in person and I did.  On arrival, a pertinent discussion was being tabled about How to improve the financial performance of our region.

The dialogue was very directional and finally, a task force was instituted headed By Eng Gilbert Mujogya Atwooki We were tasked to come up with a Road Map of TESY within  four working weeks which we ably did and presented it on Tuesday 30th May 2023 to Isaazi lya   abantu   Bakuru ba Tooro and it was very much appreciated

 From then, we set to work the bees in preparation for the symposium. The vision  had been set to take us a long way, crowned by the focus on the Industrialization of the region.

 Our first Preparation meeting was on Friday 19th May 2023 The focus was mainly on two things. District Visits and Review of the Road Map. Over 32 meetings have been held in preparation   for  this ecstatic event. We however resolved to use the event as a launch pad for the Long-term vision for an entrepreneurship forum yet to be named. A lot of work has been put into laying the  foundation of this forum to which every entrepreneur is invited to belong.

 The major purpose of it is to organize producers in their different sectors of a specific production, advocate on their behalf, train entrepreneurs on better practices, and bring them close to other production resources such as capital, social networks, and Knowledge.

   Now at the height of D-day, we are very grateful to our partners who have made our efforts worthwhile .

  The  taskforce team members wh have tirelessly put aside they time and other nesources to contribute to the success of the day (Bob, Ronald, Wily Angel, Bridget, Nice, Martin and our elders), Our financial partners such as Tooro Royal cottages who gave us the first Shs. 2m, Bank of Uganda, Post Bank, Roke Telkom, Housing Finance Bank Private sector foundation, Kalya Courts Hotel.

Njara suites, Musingi Engineering Ltd. Tsel sales connect, all our support team and well-wishers, you make a great difference in the wellbeing of our people.

 Finally, as we walk through a series of enterprising activities in the next 12 months, we ask for your continued support as we bring you the leveled value of benefit from our engagements.

   A lot is  underway for our entrepreneurs to see exponential improvements and growth in their businesses.

          The writer is the Secretary to the TESY Taskforce

    Eng. Muguda Murungi S Apuuli  `
