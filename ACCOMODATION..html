BEST ACCOMODATIONS IN THE REGION

  Sitting on the west side of the country and lying in tthe greater western arm of the east African rift valley, the region is blessed with abundant lakes, rivers,forests and parks lying in the greater national parks giving you an eye taking sceens that can bring comfort mind and heart and also soothing up your heart.

    Moving from the beautiful vast wilderness of the kabale and the mighty elizabeth park, best accomodations were built to provide a heart soothing experience during your visit in the region ranging from the mega 5 star hotels and lodges to the wild camping sites all just made to make you feel at home during your walk of adventure.

 

SEMULIKI SAFARI LODGE

 A member of the exclusive Classic Safari Camps of Africa group, located in western Uganda, on the Semliki Valley Wildlife Reserve, Semliki Safari Lodge is a luxury lodge with

reputation for providing guests with a real Ugandan bush experience.

    Overview

A stone, wood and thatch build, the lodge consists of a mess area and 8 sleeping tents. Set in a beautifully landscaped garden, the shared areas include a lounge, bar, veranda, dining

 area, library and swimming pool

Tabebuia Spa & Safari Resort

Luxury Safari & Wellness Resort in Uganda | Tabebuia Spa & Safari Resort

Welcome to Tabebuia Spa & Safari Resort, an eco-friendly luxury resort located in the heart of Uganda’s stunning wilderness. Nestled by the shores of Lake Nyabikere near Fort Portal,

 our resort offers the perfect balance of relaxation and adventure. From world-class safari experiences to rejuvenating spa treatments, Tabebuia is where luxury meets nature.
