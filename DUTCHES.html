DUTCHESS BOUTIQUE HOTEL & RESTAURANT

 Dutches boutique hotel and restaurant in Fort Portal uganda. A tranquil and culinary oasis just outside the city center.

Our food

In the restaurant we combine the fresh products Fort Portal has to offer with our home made dairy. Our excellent international cuisine serves

 breakfast,lunch and dinner.We are proud to be the first in Fort Portal to make real Italian pizza, serve cappuccinos and produce ice cream.

With serving the finest cuts of beef, chicken, crocodile and pork; all the way to our innovative vegetarian dishes we are the top destination to eat out in

 western Uganda.

Garden and fireplace

You can enjoy yourselves outside in our luscious garden, on the covered terrace or in the lounge. On cold days you can sit next to the warm and cosy fireplace in the restaurant.

Our garden becomes your own personal oasis of tranquility and relaxation while watching the many birds that visit us: the Great blue Turaco, Ross' Toraco, sunbirds, African Harrier Hawk and many others.

The restaurant is open daily from 7:00 am to 11:00 pm, the kitchen closes for last orders at 9:30 pm.
