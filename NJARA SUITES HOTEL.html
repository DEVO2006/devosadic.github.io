NJARA SUITES HOTEL

         NJARA SUITES HOTEL

    Njara suites Fort Portal is a family-run bed and breakfast hotel located in the Boma Fort Portal tourism city.

   This boutique hotel features serene sarrounding with lush gardens perfect for family gateways and travellers looking for peaceful and quite gateway. situated at the

    foot of the snowcapped Rwenzori mountains, this hotel is walking distance from the Tooro botanical gardens and Tooro gulf club and the town center

    Accommodation at Njara Suites Fort Portal, Uganda

At the hotel, each room has a wardrobe, a flat-screen TV, a private bathroom, bed linen and towels.

   There is an option of one and two bedroom apartments with a maximum occupancy of two and four people respectively. Each apartment has 2 balconies, bathroom & toilet, living & dining

   area and Kitchen. Perfect for families, long, medium & short term stay.    

   This Fort Portal apartment provides complimentary wireless Internet access. Flat-screen televisions come with cable channels. Housekeeping is provided on a limited basis.

Equipped with ironing facilities, as well as soundproofed windows, a patio and a balcony are at guests' disposal. A television makes up the in-room media amenities. Reservation Page
