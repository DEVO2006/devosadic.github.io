Tabebuia Spa & Safari Resort

Luxury Safari & Wellness Resort in Uganda | Tabebuia Spa & Safari Resort

Welcome to Tabebuia Spa & Safari Resort, an eco-friendly luxury resort located in the heart of Uganda’s stunning wilderness. Nestled by the shores of Lake Nyabikere near Fort Portal,

 our resort offers the perfect balance of relaxation and adventure. From world-class safari experiences to rejuvenating spa treatments, Tabebuia is where luxury meets nature.

        ACCOMMODATION

​

​   Luxury Accommodation Overlooking Lake Nyabikere

​  Stay in one of our luxurious, eco-conscious suites or villas, each offering breathtaking views of the lake and surrounding wildlife. Whether you’re here for a romantic getaway or a

 family adventure, our accommodations provide the perfect base for your Ugandan experience.

​

·           Lakeside Lullaby Suites: These private residences offer the utmost in privacy and comfort for longer stays.

         Whispering Lakeside Villas: Ideal for couples seeking tranquility, these suites offer private balconies with panoramic lake views.

        Lakeside Breeze Residences: Spacious and secluded, our villas are perfect for families or small groups.
