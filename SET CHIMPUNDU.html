 CHIMPUNDU LODGE

 Consisting of 14 luxury cottages, Chimpundu Lodge gives you the perfect view of the surrounding landscape and wildlife right from your own private deck.

 Enjoy the sound of wildlife in the

background, the rustling of the African trees, and the twinkling of the stars as you relax between the forest Trees in your cottages.

 Luxury cottage accommodation provides true Kibale Forrest ambiance, with every comfort catered for, including spacious dressing rooms, en-suite

 bathrooms, and private sundecks.

 All cottages are equipped with modern amenities, including fans, Wi-Fi (High-speed Wireless Internet), mini-bar, hairdryer, tea and coffee making facilities,

 and additional blankets are available on request for the chillier months.

Accomodations

Your luxury double cottage is equipped with a sitting area, King size bed, desk, fully-stocked minibar, walk-in wardrobe, and a window seat which is a day bed.

The en-suite bathroom features a walled shower and clawfoot bathtub with windows overlooking Kibale forest.
